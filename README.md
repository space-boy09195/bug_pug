# bug_pug
🪲 Welcome to the Bug Pub! 🍻
Hello! grab a cold syntax error, and take a break from the terminal.

The Bug Pub is a lightweight, real-time social lounge designed specifically for anyone to unwind, hang out, or connect. No pull requests, no failing pipelines, and absolutely no skibidi projects allowed here—just good vibes.

🎮 Features at a Glance:
-Choose Your Bug: Drop into the pub as a cozy little insect emoji (are you a feature-packed 🪲, a stealthy 🐛, or a buzzing 🐝?).

-Real-time Roaming: Move your bug freely around the pub using your arrow keys or WASD to mingle with fellow humans?

-Live Chatbox: Speak your mind! Drop a message in the chat to spawn a live bubble right over your emoji and swap your wildest debugging horror stories or just rant sum stuff.
